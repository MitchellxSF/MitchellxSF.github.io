For my Time Based Media Project, I wanted to show a catepillar's journey to become a butterfly. I worked on my project for 2 days using Adobe Animate and my animation was a length of only about 20 seconds. After completing my short, I now have a deeper appreciation of how much work and time it takes to create full-length animated shows and movies.
